Jason and Judy in the USA

Last year Jason was in England. This autumn - or this fall, as Jason would say -Judy is staying with Jason's family in Boston in the United States. Jason: So, how does it feel to be in the United States? Judy: Pretty good, actually. But you drive on the wrong side of the road. Jason: Yeah, I noticed that in England too! Any trouble with the language? Judy: No problem. I understand almost everything people say. Jason: Great! You'll soon be speaking perfect American. Judy: No way! Jason: There you are! Perfect American already.
